1. Introduction Video Tutorial

The present document describes the Mamar application which aims to provide an environment for efficient management and control of Customs Declaration Process.

The scope includes the following functionalities:

  1. Declaration
  2. Pending Transactions
  3. Automated Duty Transfer Re-Export (Maqasa)
  4. Accounting
  5. Importer Registration
  6. Agent Delegation

6.1.2.4 ATA Carnet Details

A Carnet or ATA Carnet (pronounced kar-nay) is an international customs and temporary export-import document. It is used to clear customs in 87 countries and territories without paying duties and import taxes on merchandise that will be re-exported within 12 months*. Carnets are also known as Merchandise Passports or Passports for Goods, to enter the ATA carnet details click on the ATA carnet details button of the shipment details and enter the information shown in the below screen shot.

  • Cust Bill Details: contains information related to Declaration like tariff, Excise declaration number, credit Customer etc.

6.1.4 Finalizing the Declaration

6.1.4.1 Final Validation

After entering the shipment and invoice information the user needs to click on the    button to validate the declaration, system at this stage will validate the information provided by the clearing agent and give appropriate error message to the user, after successfully clearing the system validation.

System will pop up a window with option to send the declaration to OGA’s for approval, based on the information provided in the declaration the system will enable respective send to OGA button and user should click on the respective button to send the BE information to the OGA, the system will allow the user to send the BE information to only one OGA at a time, once send to OGA button is clicked, the user will not be allowed update any information in the declaration

After receiving all the necessary approval user can apply for Pre arrival clearance by clicking on the pre clearance button, system will process the BE and assign appropriate channel to declaration based on the intelligent risk profiling.

6.1.4.2 Bill of Entry Print

After receiving all the necessary approval user can apply for Pre arrival clearance by clicking on the pre clearance button, system will process the BE and assign appropriate channel to declaration based on the intelligent risk profiling and user will be able to print the Bill of Entry and respective receipts.

7. Pre- Clearance Process Video Tutorial #back to top

7.1. Pending Transaction

Transaction → Pre Clearance Process → Pending Transaction

Pending transaction feature enables the user to process the declaration where the bill of entry has not been generated either due to channeling or due to other reasons, such as insufficient facility balance at time of pre clearance of the job.

7.1.1. Yellow Channel Process

First tab of the screen is used to process the Bill of Entries which are classified as Yellow channel and the channel have been overridden or re-classified to either red or green channel by the customs officer. On processing such declaration from this screen the system would generate the bill of entry and the corresponding receipt voucher for duty and other declaration related charges.

7.1.2. Pending Bills

The Pending bill tab is used to process the declaration which have not been processed due to reasons, such as insufficient facility balance at time of pre clearance of the job On processing such declaration from this screen the system would generate the bill of entry and the corresponding receipt voucher for duty and other declaration related charges.
